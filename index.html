Hips Don't Lie

How Music and Virtual Reality Shape Body Sway and Balance

Have you ever felt unsteady on your feet? What if music could change how well you balance? Scroll to explore how music and virtual reality influences our body's stability and sway patterns.

Background

This study examined how music and virtual reality affects body sway, which is the natural movement of the body while standing. 28 participants stood on a balance platform that measured their Center Of Pressure (COP) movement in the x and y directions, indicating how much the body moved.

They experienced different combinations of visual conditions (eyes closed, looking at VR) and music conditions (listening to Mozart's Jupiter Symphony).

Researchers hypothesized that music and VR motion would increase body sway, potentially providing insights into new balance training methods for older adults and individuals at risk of falls. Keep reading to see the results!
